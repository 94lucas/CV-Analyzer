# Resume and Job Description Analyzer Chatbot

This chatbot analyzes resumes (CVs) and job descriptions to assess the likelihood of a match and identify areas for improvement for the applicant.

**Key Features:**

* **Match Likelihood Analysis:** Evaluates the alignment between the skills and experience presented in a resume and the requirements outlined in a job description.
* **Points for Improvement:** Provides specific feedback on areas where the resume could be strengthened to better align with the target job.
* **User-Friendly Chatbot Interface:** Offers an intuitive way for users to interact with the analysis tool.

**Potential Use Cases:**

* Job seekers looking to tailor their resumes for specific roles.
* Individuals seeking to understand their strengths and weaknesses in relation to job market demands.
* Career advisors assisting candidates in optimizing their application materials.
